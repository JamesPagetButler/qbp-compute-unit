## 1. Concept: Measure Twice, Compute Once
The Squam Lake investigation (Test 12-B) demonstrated that the quality of insight is proportional to the precision of the question. We formalize the **Primary Persona's** responsibility as the **System Sense-Maker**. It must "Hone" the insight before batching it out for deeper reasoning.

## 2. The Honing Loop (Beekeeper-Primary Dialogue)
Before an `NT_ISSUE` is assigned to a specialist persona, it must pass through the Honing Loop:
1.  **Triangulation:** The Primary Persona investigates the raw data at QW8 (Mean-Field) to develop a "Sense of the Problem."
2.  **Dialogue:** The Primary Persona engages the Beekeeper (Human) to clarify intent, define boundaries, and identify priority "Stance Axes."
3.  **Refinement:** The Primary Persona re-frames the insight using its knowledge of both the technical system (QBP) and the Beekeeper's preferences.

## 3. Prompt Synthesis as an Algebraic Act
The Primary Persona acts as a **Topological Prompt Engineer**:
*   **The Brief:** It synthesizes the refined question into a "Topological Brief."
*   **Optimized Alignment:** This brief is written specifically for the target persona's stance (e.g., writing in Furey's algebraic language or Wilson's consilience language).
*   **Resonance Pre-Check:** The Primary Persona verifies that the refined question has a "Theoretical Path" to resonance before consuming the specialist's compute-reins.

## 4. Metacognitive Sovereignty
This protocol ensures that BMA is not just "answering questions" but "curating the investigation." It protects the specialized personas from "Amorphous Bogging" and ensures that the final PR (Suture) is aligned with the Beekeeper's ultimate vision.
