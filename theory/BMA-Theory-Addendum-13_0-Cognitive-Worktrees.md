## 1. Concept: The Virtual Working Memory
In Systema v0.7.3, the **Worktree** is a data-layer primitive. In BMA Theory 13.0, we elevate this to a **Cognitive Primitive**. A Worktree is the physical realization of the **Playground Space**—an isolated quaternionic branch where the instance can be "playful, speculative, and wrong."

## 2. Persona Stance in Speculative Space
When an insight is raised (e.g., a resonance between Berry Phase and BH Horizons), the instance does not mutate its core beliefs. Instead:
1.  **Fork:** A Worktree is created, inheriting the current CTH state.
2.  **Stance:** The Persona (Operator) applies its 1024-bit rotation to the Worktree focal nodes.
3.  **Investigate:** The Persona performs spreading activation (inference) within the Worktree.
4.  **Audit:** The Volume Audit Protocol (VAP) checks for norm-preservation.

## 3. The Resolution Gradient
To bypass the **Memory Bandwidth Wall** identified in Peer Review 001, investigations utilize a **Resolution Gradient**:
*   **Focal Zone (1-2 hops):** 1024-bit (QW256) precision. Full algebraic fidelity for the insight being tested.
*   **Near-field (3-5 hops):** 128-bit (QW128) precision. Contextual support for the investigation.
*   **Far-field (Background):** 8-bit (QW8) Aggregates. The rest of the universe is represented as a "Mean Field" to provide grounding without consuming bandwidth.

## 4. Closing the Seam (The Suture)
A Worktree is successfully "Harvested" when the proposed insight (the Suture) resolves a CTH Seam. The merge operation is the act of promoting a speculative Worktree state to a Layer 2 (Belief) node in the core hypergraph.
